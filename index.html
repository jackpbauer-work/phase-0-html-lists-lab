Grilled Cheese Sandwhich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Instructions

  1. 1 Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. 2 Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. 3 Add cheese on top of bread
  4. 4 Cover with second slice of bread
  5. 5 Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
